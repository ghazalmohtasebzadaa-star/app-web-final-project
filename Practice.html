Practice

Ghazal Architectural Office operates as a multidisciplinary architectural practice focused on the design of residential, cultural, and commercial projects. The practice emphasizes a context-sensitive approach, integrating architectural form, function, and environmental performance.The studio’s design methodology is based on careful site analysis, spatial clarity, and material honesty. Each project is developed through a rigorous design process that balances aesthetic expression with structural logic and sustainability principles.From early conceptual design to detailed architectural drawings, the practice aims to deliver thoughtful and innovative solutions that respond to both client needs and cultural context.
